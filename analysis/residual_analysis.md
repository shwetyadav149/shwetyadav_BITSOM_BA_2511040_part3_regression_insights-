# Residual Analysis

Residuals were calculated as:

Residual = Actual Sales - Predicted Sales

## Positive Residual Stores

Stores with large positive residuals performed better than expected.

Possible reasons:

* Local promotions
* Festivals
* Better customer service
* Strong store management

## Negative Residual Stores

Stores with large negative residuals underperformed compared to model expectations.

Possible reasons:

* Inventory shortages
* Increased local competition
* Operational inefficiencies

## Model Bias Assessment

The model does not show systematic overprediction or underprediction but some store categories exhibit larger residuals.

# Final Recommendation

## Key Drivers of Sales

The analysis indicates that the strongest drivers of monthly sales are:

* Marketing Spend
* Customer Footfall
* Inventory Availability
* Customer Rating

## Recommended Business Actions

1. Increase investment in effective marketing channels.
2. Improve store traffic generation strategies.
3. Maintain inventory availability above target levels.
4. Improve customer experience initiatives.

## Variables That Should Not Be Over-Interpreted

* Competitor Distance
* Some Regional Effects
* Certain Store Type Effects

## Risks and Limitations

Regression identifies relationships between variables but cannot establish causality.

External factors such as local events, economic conditions and seasonality may influence sales outcomes.

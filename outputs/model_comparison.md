# Model Comparison

| Model               | Variables Used                | R-Squared | Business Usefulness |
| ------------------- | ----------------------------- | --------- | ------------------- |
| Simple Regression 1 | Marketing Spend               | Lower     | Limited             |
| Simple Regression 2 | Footfall                      | Lower     | Moderate            |
| Multiple Regression | Multiple Predictors + Dummies | Highest   | High                |

## Findings

The multiple regression model explained a significantly larger proportion of sales variation compared to the simple regression models.

## Limitations

* The model does not capture seasonality.
* External economic factors are not included.
* Correlation does not imply causation.
